Is Your Lactating Sow Farm Facing These Challenges?

High piglet mortality & poor uniformity

Industry average lactating mortality 8-15%. Weak/crush deaths and runt piglets cost more than the feed savings. Grade A weaner rate below 80% means every piglet is worth less at sale.

Sow feed intake drops, body condition lost

Lactating sows need 6-8kg/day but often eat less. Body condition loss ≥1 BCS unit delays return to estrus by 3-7 days, reducing annual litter rate from 2.3 to ≤2.1.

Inadequate IgG transfer in first 6h leaves piglets vulnerable. Gut development in week 1-2 determines lifetime performance — poor nutrition here creates permanent FCR handicap.
