为什么活力得®枯草菌源蛋白酶有效？

①

胎盘营养传输优化

活力得®枯草菌源蛋白酶调节T3和生长激素（GH）分泌，上调GLUT-4表现，强化胎盘绒毛膜发育，提升氨基酸跨膜转运效率（PMC 2018）

→ 同样的饲料，更多营养穿过胎盘到达胎儿。胎儿长得更均匀，弱仔自然减少。

②

抗氧化胎儿保护

活力得®枯草菌源蛋白酶精准激活Nrf2/ARE抗氧化通路，清除活性氧（ROS），降低胚胎氧化损伤，保护细胞膜完整性，降低早期胚胎死亡率（PMC 2017）

→ 怀孕前期胚胎最脆弱。活力得®枯草菌源蛋白酶帮胚胎挡住自由基攻击，着床后存活率更高。

③

母猪能量代谢平衡

上调AMPK通路，优化葡萄糖代谢，维持孕期体况（BCS 2.5-3.5），降低亚临床炎症，减少代谢病风险

→ 母猪孕期不容易过胖或过瘦。体况稳定，下一胎繁殖成绩更有保障。

Why Does VitalBoost® Protease Work?

①

Placental nutrient transport optimization

VitalBoost® Protease regulates T3 and GH secretion, upregulates GLUT-4, enhances placental development and amino acid transport (PMC 2018).

→ Same feed, more nutrients cross the placenta to reach piglets. More uniform growth, fewer weak ones.

②

Antioxidant fetal protection

Activates Nrf2/ARE pathway, scavenges ROS, reduces oxidative damage to embryos, protects cell membrane integrity (PMC 2017).

→ Early embryos are fragile. VitalBoost® shields them from oxidative damage, improving implantation survival.

③

Sow energy metabolism balance

Upregulates AMPK, optimizes glucose metabolism, maintains BCS 2.5-3.5, reduces subclinical inflammation and metabolic disease risk.

→ Keeps sows in optimal body condition throughout gestation — not too fat, not too thin.
