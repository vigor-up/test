为什么活力得®枯草菌源蛋白酶有效？

①

肠道消化酶激活

活力得®枯草菌源蛋白酶高效分解大分子蛋白质为小肽和氨基酸，提升肠道吸收利用率（PMC 2018）

→ 同样的饲料，猪吸收更多蛋白质。料少，长得一样快。FCR直接改善。

②

脂肪代谢重塑

精准调控脂肪合成/分解代谢通路，促进脂肪向肌肉转化，降低皮下脂肪沉积

→ 脂肪不堆背上，转成肌肉。背脂从2.5cm降到2cm以下，出肉率自然提升。

③

线粒体ATP合成强化

嵌入肝脏线粒体膜，激活细胞色素c氧化酶，ATP合成效率提升25-30%（MDPI 2014）

→ 猪的细胞产能更高效。每克饲料产生更多能量，肌肉生长加速。

Why Does VitalBoost® Protease Work?

①

Gut Enzyme Activation

VitalBoost® Protease efficiently breaks down macromolecular proteins into small peptides and amino acids, increasing absorption (PMC 2018).

→ Same feed, more protein absorbed. Less feed needed for same growth. FCR improves directly.

②

Fat Metabolism Remodeling

Precise regulation of fat synthesis/breakdown pathways, redirecting fat to muscle, reducing subcutaneous fat deposition.

→ Fat goes to muscle, not backfat. Backfat drops below 2cm. Carcass yield rises.

③

Mitochondrial ATP Boost

Targets liver mitochondrial membrane, activates cytochrome c oxidase, ATP synthesis +25-30% (MDPI 2014).

→ Pig cells generate energy more efficiently. More growth per gram of feed.
