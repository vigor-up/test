行业现状

您的虾场是否也面临这些挑战？

2024年行业背景

全球白对虾产量突破650万吨，虾价持续承压。饲料占养殖成本50–60%，低存活率+高FCR的「双杀」让虾场利润几乎清零。EMS（急性肝胰腺坏死病）自2010年累计造成全球损失超440亿美元，弧菌慢性感染更是每批次隐性损耗的主因。

EMS急性肝胰腺坏死

投苗后10–35天死亡率可达100%。由副溶血弧菌（V. parahaemolyticus）产生毒素摧毁肝胰腺，广东、海南、越南、泰国无一幸免。

弧菌病+慢性损耗

即使没有EMS爆发，弧菌长期慢性感染也让FCR从1.1涨到1.4，存活率从70%掉到55%，出虾规格不均匀，只能低价处理。

WSSV白斑病毒

白斑综合征（WSSV）感染后72小时内死亡率可达100%。斑节对虾和罗氏沼虾尤其敏感，高密度养殖场传播速度极快。

生物机制

三层分子保护机制——为什么枯草芽孢杆菌能同时解决FCR与存活率

给配方师的技术摘要：对虾属甲壳类无脊椎动物，免疫系统以先天性免疫为主（无适应性免疫），主要依赖血细胞吞噬、酚氧化酶（PO）级联反应及抗氧化防御系统。枯草芽孢杆菌（Bacillus subtilis）在虾肠道中通过三条路径协同发挥作用：①蛋白酶水解提升消化吸收效率（FCR↓15.1%，PMID:38481478）；②免疫基因上调增强先天免疫（Vibrio感染存活率21%→61%，APJMBB 2024）；③Nrf2/KEAP1通路激活强化抗氧化屏障（SOD↑33%，存活率97.4%）。三路径叠加效果远超单一药物干预。
①

蛋白质精准水解·消化效率+49.3%

枯草芽孢杆菌产生的碱性蛋白酶（EC 3.4.21.62）在虾消化道中高效水解植物性蛋白质中的抗营养因子，将大分子蛋白切割为2–6残基短肽，提升小肠刷状缘二肽酶效率。饲料利用率+49.3%，运营成本↓20.2%（PMID:38481478）。

→ FCR 1.32→1.12（↓15.1%），每造直接节省饲料成本，比添加成本高10倍以上。

PMID:38481478；APJMBB 2024 (FCR 1.53±0.10)

②

免疫基因上调·弧菌感染存活↑187%

枯草芽孢杆菌定殖肠道后，上调虾免疫相关基因：Hsp70（热休克蛋白，应激保护）、LGBP（脂多糖结合蛋白，细菌识别）、PO（酚氧化酶，杀菌）。副溶血弧菌（V. parahaemolyticus）感染存活率从对照组21.33%提升至61.33%（+187.5%，APJMBB 2024）；TCBS培养板弧菌菌落数↓95%（ResearchGate 2026）。

→ 弧菌是虾场头号杀手。先天免疫防线增强后，EMS爆发风险显著下降，慢性弧菌损耗大幅减少。

APJMBB 2024；ResearchGate 2026；水生生物学报2025

③

Nrf2/KEAP1抗氧化·存活率97.4%

枯草芽孢杆菌代谢产物激活Nrf2-KEAP1通路，上调超氧化物歧化酶（SOD）、过氧化氢酶（CAT）及谷胱甘肽过氧化物酶（GPx）。血淋巴SOD活性↑33%；肠道绒毛高度与密度提升，肠道屏障完整性改善；在高密度RAS系统中存活率达97.4%（PMID:38481478）。WSSV攻毒存活率提升+10%（MDPI 2023）。

→ 应激（高密度/换水/天气/运输）是存活率波动的根源。SOD提升意味着虾更抗压、恢复更快，高密度场效益尤为显著。

PMID:38481478；MDPI 2023；FSI 2025

Mechanism

Three-Layer Molecular Protection — Why Bacillus subtilis Solves Both FCR and Survival Rate

Technical summary for aquaculture nutritionists: Shrimp rely exclusively on innate immunity (no adaptive immune system) — hemocyte phagocytosis, prophenoloxidase (proPO) cascade, and antioxidant defense are the only lines of defense. Bacillus subtilis operates through three parallel pathways: ① alkaline protease (EC 3.4.21.62) improves feed digestibility (+49.3%, PMID:38481478); ② immune gene upregulation enhances innate defense (Vibrio survival 21%→61%, APJMBB 2024); ③ Nrf2/KEAP1 pathway activation strengthens antioxidant barrier (SOD↑33%, survival 97.4%). The synergistic effect of all three pathways far exceeds any single pharmaceutical intervention.
①

Protein Hydrolysis · Feed Utilization +49.3%

Subtilisin (EC 3.4.21.62) efficiently hydrolyzes anti-nutritional factors in plant-based proteins, cleaving large molecules into 2–6 residue peptides and improving brush-border dipeptidase efficiency. Feed utilization +49.3%, operating cost ↓20.2% (PMID:38481478). APJMBB 2024: FCR 1.53±0.10 (probiotic group) vs higher in controls.

→ FCR 1.32→1.12 (↓15.1%). Feed savings per crop exceed enhancer cost by 10×+.

Immune Gene Upregulation · Vibrio Survival +187%

Bacillus subtilis colonization upregulates shrimp immune genes: Hsp70 (stress protection), LGBP (bacterial recognition), PO (bactericidal cascade). V. parahaemolyticus challenge survival: control (K+) 21.33% vs probiotic group 61.33% (+187.5%, APJMBB 2024). TCBS plate Vibrio colonies ↓95% (ResearchGate 2026).

→ Vibrio is the #1 killer in shrimp ponds. Stronger innate immunity means significantly reduced EMS outbreak risk and chronic Vibrio losses.

Nrf2/KEAP1 Antioxidant · Survival Rate 97.4%

Metabolites activate Nrf2-KEAP1 pathway, upregulating SOD, CAT, and GPx. Hemolymph SOD activity ↑33%; intestinal villi height and density increase, improving gut barrier integrity. In high-density RAS systems, survival rate reaches 97.4% (PMID:38481478). WSSV challenge survival ↑10% (MDPI 2023).

→ Stress (high density/water exchange/weather) drives survival rate volatility. SOD↑33% means shrimp resist stress better and recover faster.
